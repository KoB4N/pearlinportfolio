About

Pearlin Teo is a Singapore-based architect specializing in creating spaces that balance functionality with aesthetic excellence. With a focus on sustainable design and human-centric spaces, each project reflects a unique dialogue between form and environment.

The approach combines contemporary design principles with timeless elements, resulting in spaces that are both innovative and enduring.

Design Philosophy

Each project is approached with a fresh perspective, yet maintains a cohesive design language. The work emphasizes:

  • Harmony with surrounding landscapes
  • Sustainable material choices
  • Thoughtful spatial planning
  • Timeless aesthetic appeal
