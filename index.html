RESIDENTIAL CASE PROJECT IN PARIS

This residential project in Paris showcases Pearlin's ability to blend modern design with classic Parisian architecture. The project features open spaces, natural light optimization, and sustainable materials.

Completed in 2020, this project received the European Architecture Award for residential design.

×

COMFORT FULL IN NEW YORK

The Comfort Full project in New York redefines urban living with its innovative use of space and materials. Pearlin's design focuses on creating a sense of comfort and community in a high-rise environment.

Key features include communal green spaces, smart home technology integration, and energy-efficient systems.

×

MURDER HOTEL IN LONDON

This controversial yet award-winning project transformed a historic London building into a boutique hotel with a unique theme. Pearlin's design preserved the building's heritage while creating a modern, immersive experience.

The project was featured in Architectural Digest and won the 2021 London Design Award for hospitality spaces.
